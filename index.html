Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tickets, schedule, and what to expect at DevConf 2026.

What's included in my ticket price?
Every ticket includes full access to all keynote sessions, breakout talks, the expo floor, and catered lunch on both days. Workshop seats are limited and included only with Pro and Team passes.
Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket?
Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event and freely transferable to a colleague at any time. Requests inside the 30-day window are handled case by case — just reach out to our support team.
Will sessions be recorded and available afterward?
Yes. All keynote and breakout sessions are recorded and added to your attendee dashboard within 48 hours. Pro and Team passes include lifetime access; Standard passes include 30 days of access.
Do you offer discounts for teams or students?
Teams of 5 or more save automatically through our Team pass. Verified students and open-source maintainers get 50% off — email support with your verification after registering to receive an adjusted invoice.
Can I get an invitation letter for a visa application?
Absolutely. Once your ticket is confirmed, request a visa invitation letter from your attendee dashboard and we'll issue one within 3 business days.
Is there a code of conduct for the event?
Yes — DevConf follows a strict code of conduct to keep the event welcoming and safe for everyone. Read the full policy on our Code of Conduct page before you arrive.
